Holy Guacamole! Avocado, the green gold of the world.

An Analysis into the economic growth of a humble green fruit.


Have you ever wondered where all the avocado come from?

In one week alone, the united states recorded 16.4 million kilograms of avocado arriving in the U.S market, they themselves produces 6 million kilograms in addition to what's coming in, totalling a whooping 22.6 million kilograms in a week - and that's not even the highest number recorded. In this analysis we will endeavour to investigate where's the demand and who's fulfilling it.
